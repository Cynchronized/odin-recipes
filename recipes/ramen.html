Ramen

Description

a Japanese noodle soup, with a combination of a rich flavoured broth, one of a variety of types of noodle and a selection of meats or vegetables, often topped with a boiled egg.

Ingredients

  • Ramen Noodles
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Chicken or Vegetable Broth
  • Dried shiitake mushrooms
  • Veggies like carrots kale
  • Topping like eggs, panko, chilli oil, etc

Steps

  1. Stir fry aromatics
  2. Make broth
  3. Add noodles
  4. Add veg
  5. Top it off
  6. Profit
